Contextual backlinks can enhance internal linking, helping search engines crawl and index your site more effectively. This can distribute link equity across your pages, improving their overall SEO.

![backlink-usage-example@1.5x.png](/design/backlink-usage-example1.5x.png)

1. Underlined backlink hover state
2. The contextual backlink should follow this format: "\<- Back to Page title".
